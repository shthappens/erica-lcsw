What to Expect

Prior to your first session please call your insurance to inquire about your benefits, coverage, copay, and preauthorization. Please arrive 10-15min early for your first appointment to complete intake paperwork or print forms ahead of time and bring them completed to your first session. Sessions are 50-55 minutes.

During the first session we will get to know each other and find out more about what you are hoping to achieve in counseling.  I look forward to meeting you!

Adults

Please print and complete: 

  • Therapist-client agreement
  • Permission to Treat
  • Client Information
  • Electronic Communication Policy
  • HIPAA Notice
  • ACOK Adult
  • GAD-7
  • PHQ-9 Screen

Teens

Please print and complete with parental assistance:

  • Therapist-client agreement
  • Permission to Treat
  • Client Information
  • Developmental History
  • Electronic Communication Policy
  • HIPAA Notice
  • ACOK Adolescent
  • GAD-7
  • PHQ-9 Screen
